ABOUT

The FU is a monthly online magazine, written by young professionals and thinkers from around the world.

As our motto ‘game-plans for dreamers’ suggests, this publication is about guiding other imaginative people towards making their imaginings reality – with a combination of shared hopes, ideas and opinions, followed by concrete advice about how to do something with the topic being discussed.

Our philosophy is Informed Idealism. This means firstly, understanding the small print behind big dreams – and secondly, believing that the future is bright… because we will make it so.
